Founder and Lead Consultant

Tom O'Brien has over 26 years of experience bringing new technology products and services to market in the US and abroad.

Tom founded Bootstrap Technology to bring his business experience and his hands on development skills to help small and medium sizes businesses around the world with their technology and business challenges. His objective is to keep development efficient and focused, while managing limited resources during a company's key growth phase. Tom is actively involved in all Bootstrap Technology engagements and brings his personal and professional experience to each and every project.

Proficient in Ruby on Rails, iOS, Android, .net, C#, Salesforce.com, HTML, WordPress, PHP.

He is the co-founder,Director, and Global of Data Insurance Licensing Ltd., a New Zealand based, insurance/technology start-up focused on data protection and risk management. Data Insurance is a unique solution that combines insurance, backup management and internet technology and extensive middleware to manage policy and technical implementations. More...

As a co-founder and long-time CEO of CRS Temporary Housing, the leader in temporary housing services to the insurance industry, he led the company to revenues of $100MM. He also managed the development of a next generation web portal, myhotelsource.com, exclusively for the insurance claim industry, world-wide.

Tom was the key developer and designer of PlaceTrak, an internal .net application used to manage the temporary housing business. This product was migrated to asp.net and to the ultimately to the salesforce.com platform.

He is the co-founder and developer of Virtual Backup, one of the first online back-up systems for the small business market.

Co-inventor on two patent applications "Systems and Methods of Data Insurance" and "Electronic Data Insurance Management System and Methods".

Why Call it Bootstrap?

More than the definition and the way it's used development circles, bootstrap invokes for me a spirit of can do and self reliance. Being an entrepreneur or business owner takes grit, determination and every now and then the need and ability to pull yourself up by them. A bootstrap is only helpful on a boot, and boots are a strong symbol of the southwest. From cowboys to bikers, boots are valuable and sturdy companion. I want my clients to think of bootstrap technology the same way I think about my boots. Dependable, comfortable, daring, and outside the norm.
